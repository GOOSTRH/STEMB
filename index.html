About Cultural Heritage

Cultural heritage encompasses the traditions, customs, beliefs, and artifacts that are passed down from generation to generation. It includes everything from language and religion to music, art, and cuisine. Understanding different cultures helps us appreciate the diversity of human experience and fosters mutual respect and understanding.
